About US

Welcome to Garden Revival System

where innovation meets environmental responsibility. Our mission is to transform public parks into cleaner, greener spaces by leveraging advanced image processing technology to detect and manage garbage effectively. We believe that clean parks are not just a symbol of community pride but also vital for promoting public health, biodiversity, and sustainable urban living. Our cutting-edge system identifies garbage in public parks and provides actionable insights to park authorities. By analyzing the frequency and patterns of garbage accumulation, we determine how often maintenance is required, enabling efficient scheduling and resource allocation. But we don’t stop there. Our solution dives deeper to identify the root causes of littering and suggests targeted actions to mitigate these issues, fostering a long-term culture of cleanliness and care. At Garden Revival System, we are committed to empowering communities, environmentalists, and government agencies with the tools and data they need to make a real difference. Join us in our journey to restore the beauty of public parks and create a cleaner, healthier world for everyone. Together, we can revive nature, one park at a time!
